Assay vs MCP Servers

One 9 MB binary replaces dozens of MCP server processes. 25 domains fully covered, 15+ coming soon.

Size comparison: 10 MCP servers ≈ 500 MB npm deps + 10 processes. Assay = 9 MB + 1 process.
